#### 2.2 Admin Policy Page (SSD §13.4)

#### 2.3 Enhanced Privacy Policy Management (SSD §8.5, §9)
- **Gap**: SSD includes comprehensive admin privacy policy endpoints not covered in test plan:

  - `POST /api/admin/privacy-policies/{id}/publish` → publish policy
  - `GET /api/admin/privacy-policies` → list all policies
  - `GET /api/admin/privacy-policies/{id}` → get policy details
- **Missing**: Detailed test scenarios for policy versioning, publishing workflow, and unpublishing behavior
